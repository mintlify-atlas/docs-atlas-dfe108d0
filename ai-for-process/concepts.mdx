Understand how AI for Process enables organizations to automate complex business processes.

## Workflows

Design and deploy AI-powered workflows with a visual builder. Combine AI nodes, integrations, and human-in-the-loop steps for end-to-end process automation.

- **Visual designer**: Drag-and-drop workflow creation on a no-code canvas.
- **Pre-built nodes**: AI, integration, logic, and utility nodes.
- **Branching**: Conditional paths and loops.
- **Error handling**: Built-in retry and fallback logic.

## Workflow nodes

Use pre-built AI nodes for text generation, image processing, document intelligence, and more. Connect nodes to build sophisticated automation pipelines.

| Node type | Function |
|---|---|
| **Text-to-Text** | Generation, summarization, extraction, and translation. |
| **Text-to-Image** | Image generation from text prompts. |
| **Image-to-Text** | OCR, image description, and visual analysis. |
| **Audio-to-Text** | Transcription and speech recognition. |
| **Doc Intelligence** | Document parsing and structured data extraction. |

## Inbox

Human-in-the-loop request management. Review, approve, and provide input for workflow decisions that require human judgment.

- **Approval workflows**: Human sign-off on AI decisions.
- **Review queues**: Centralized inbox for pending tasks.
- **Escalation**: Route complex cases to subject matter experts.
- **Feedback capture**: Improve AI outputs with human corrections.

## Model Hub

Access open-source models, deploy fine-tuned models, and connect external model providers. Full control over the AI model lifecycle.

- **Open-source models**: Deploy from 30+ platform-hosted models or import from Hugging Face.
- **Fine-tuning**: Train models on your proprietary data.
- **External providers**: Connect OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Cohere, and Amazon Bedrock.
- **Model versioning**: Track and deploy model versions with full deployment history.

## Prompt Studio

Design, test, and manage prompts across the full lifecycle. Features include:

- 65+ pre-built templates for common use cases.
- Multi-model comparison across up to 5 models simultaneously.
- Variable support for batch testing.
- Version control and draft history.
- AI-assisted prompt generation.
