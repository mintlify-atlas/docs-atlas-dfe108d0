---
title: "SLAs and alerts"
description: "Set acceptable thresholds for KPIs and configure notifications when contact center metrics exceed targets."
---

SLAs & Alerts let you set acceptable thresholds for key performance indicators (KPIs) — such as Average Speed to Answer, Response Service Level, Abandonment Rate, and Transfer Rate — across voice and chat interactions.

To access, go to **Contact Center AI** > **Performance Management** > **SLAs & Alerts**.

## Service level

### Create a service level

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a new service level">
    Select **+ New Service Level** in the upper-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter name and description">
    In the **New Service Level Rule** panel, enter a **Name** and **Description**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure setup">
    Under **Configuration Setup**, select the **Queues** and **Channels**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a trigger">
    Choose from:
    - **Abandonment Rate**
    - **Average Speed to Answer (ASA)**
    - **Response Service Level (RSL)**
    - **Transfer Rate**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an action">
    Select **+ Add an action** and choose:
    - **Alert** — shows a dashboard popup.
    - **Email** — sends an email notification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Select **Save**. A confirmation message appears and the service level is created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Edit a service level

1. Select **Edit** next to the service level.
2. Make your changes and select **Save**.

### Delete a service level

1. Select **Delete** next to the service level.
2. Confirm by selecting **Delete**.

## General alerts

General Alerts let administrators and supervisors set up notifications for operational metrics and system events.

### Create a general alert

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a new alert">
    Select the **General Alerts** tab, then select **+ New Alert** in the upper-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter name and description">
    In the **New General Alert** panel, enter a **Name** and **Description**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a module">
    Under **Configuration Setup**, select a module:

    - **After Call Work (ACW)** — select queues, channels, and duration.
    - **Analytics > Interactions** — trigger on export events (Interaction Details Report, Interaction Dashboard, Interaction Details by Segment).
    - **Voicemail in Agent Console** — trigger on voicemail count within a time interval.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add an action">
    Select **+ Add an action** and choose **Alert** or **Email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Select **Save**. A notification appears and the alert is created.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Edit a general alert

1. Select **Edit** next to the alert.
2. Make your changes and select **Save**.

### Delete a general alert

1. Select **Delete** next to the alert.
2. Confirm by selecting **Delete**.
