**Plan and Usage** lets you monitor billing, track app usage, manage subscriptions, and access invoices — all from a single location.

To access it, navigate to **Product Switcher > Billing > Manage > Plan and Usage**.

## Plans

| Plan | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Essential** | Core features to get started building an AI chatbot. |
| **Advanced** | Everything in Essential, plus higher limits and more advanced features. |
| **Enterprise** | Everything in Advanced, plus enterprise features, custom pricing, and the highest limits. |

## How billing works

### Automation AI

Billed per **15-minute billing session** of user conversation. A 31-minute conversation = 3 sessions (0–15 min, 16–30 min, 31–end).

### Search AI

Included in Automation AI; uses the same 15-minute billing session.

### Contact Center AI

Billed per **agent seat**:

| Seat type | How it's counted |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Named Seats** | One seat per agent, regardless of shifts. |
| **Concurrent Seats** | Based on the maximum number of agents logged in simultaneously. |

### Agent AI

Billed per agent seat, same as Contact Center AI.

## Plan information

The **Plan Information** section shows your current plan and product usage. Select **Subscribe** to subscribe to a new plan or **Modify** to change your current plan.

## Usage trends

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Billing Sessions** | Total billing sessions completed. |
| **Voice Sessions** | Total voice interactions, including STT, TTS, and Voice Gateway calls. |
| **Agent Count** | Concurrent agent seats subscribed for the selected period. |

Filter by individual app, or select a predefined range (24 hours, 7 days, 30 days, or 90 days) or custom date range.

## Payment methods

| Method | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **PayPal** | Link your PayPal email account to the payment gateway. |
| **Credit / Debit Card** | Enter your card number, expiry date, and CVV. Confirm with an OTP. |
| **Wire Transfer** | Transfer funds directly from one bank account to another. Takes 4–8 business days. |

## Subscribe or modify a plan

1. Navigate to **Manage > Plan & Usage**.
2. Select **Subscribe** (for a new plan) or **Modify** (to change your current plan).
3. Select a plan and select **Proceed and Modify**.
4. Enter your **Billing Information**, select a **Payment Method**, and enter payment details.
5. Select **Submit Order**.

## Invoices

Invoices are available for Standard Accounts only.

1. Log in to the XO Platform and select the app.
2. Navigate to **Product Switcher > Billing > Manage > My Invoices**.
3. Invoices are listed in chronological order. Select the download icon to export as PDF.

## Email notifications

The platform automatically sends emails for key events including subscriptions, renewal confirmations, trial expirations, and failed payments.
