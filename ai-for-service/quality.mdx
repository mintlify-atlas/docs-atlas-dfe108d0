Quality AI enables automated quality management and conversation analysis across your contact center.

## Overview

Quality AI enables you to:

- Evaluate 100% of customer interactions.
- Identify coaching opportunities automatically.
- Monitor compliance and adherence.
- Drive continuous improvement with data.

## How it works

1. **Speech/text analysis** — transcribe and analyze conversations from voice, chat, email, and social channels.
2. **Evaluation scoring** — map conversation content to evaluation criteria and assign scores.
3. **Confidence flagging** — flag low-confidence scores for human review.
4. **Insight generation** — surface coaching assignments, compliance alerts, and performance trends.

## Evaluation criteria

### Standard criteria

| Criteria | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Greeting** | Proper introduction and identification. |
| **Empathy** | Acknowledging customer emotions. |
| **Issue understanding** | Correctly identifying the problem. |
| **Resolution** | Providing accurate solution. |
| **Closing** | Proper wrap-up and next steps. |
| **Compliance** | Following required disclosures. |

### Evaluation forms

Group criteria into evaluation forms and apply them to queues with a pass threshold (for example, 80%).

## Auto-scoring

AI evaluates interactions in four steps:

1. Speech/text analysis — transcribe and analyze the conversation.
2. Criteria matching — map conversation content to evaluation criteria.
3. Scoring — assign scores based on evidence found.
4. Confidence flagging — flag low-confidence scores for human review.

## Coaching

Trigger coaching automatically based on evaluation scores. Each coaching assignment includes the conversation transcript, audio recording, evaluation scorecard, and specific timestamps.

[Learn more about coaching →](/ai-for-service/quality-ai/coaching)

## Setup Quality AI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Configure permissions">
    Go to **User Management** > **Role Management** > **New Role** > **Other Modules** and assign QM permissions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up contact center">
    Assign Supervisors and Auditors to the relevant queues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable features">
    Enable **Conversation Intelligence**, **Auto QA**, and **Bookmarks** in Quality AI **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create evaluation metrics">
    Choose a measurement type and create evaluation metrics.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create evaluation forms">
    Assign a name, description, channel, and pass score. Select metrics, assign weights, and link the form to queues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analyze interactions">
    Use Conversation Mining to review scored interactions and create audit allocations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor performance">
    Use the **Dashboard** and **Conversation Intelligence Dashboard** to track quality trends.
  </Step>
</Steps>
